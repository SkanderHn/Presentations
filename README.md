# Presentations
Presentations on the topic of ML that I have given to my colleagues or to outside teams. 

No actual code, just power points. 

DS_ReadingGroup_AIC_BIC_Public.pptx is on the topic of alternatives to out of sample testing, based more or less on the content of ESL Chapter 7, by Hastie and Tibshirani. 
DS_ReadingGroup_MCMC_Public.pptx is an intuitive explanation of MCMC methods, along with how they pretain to the Facebook Prophet and BSTS times series tools. 
NNets_Demand_Forecasting_Public.pptx is a presentation on the suitability of deep learning methods for demand forecasting (as compared to statistical methods).
